Multiple Player Types

Kaltura supports multiple player types designed for different use cases—video, audio, quizzes, interactive experiences, and more. Each player comes with a rich set of features that can be enabled, configured, or extended to match specific technical, branding, and user experience requirements.

This flexibility allows organizations to tailor playback behavior, interactivity, performance optimizations, and integrations without compromising consistency or scalability. Whether the goal is faster page loads, deeper engagement, or specialized learning and marketing experiences, Kaltura players can be customized to fit a wide range of workflows and audience needs.

Video Player without Autoplay

The Kaltura Video Player is highly customizable, allowing organizations to adapt the player to their exact technical, branding, and user experience requirements. From UI layout, controls, and themes to playback behavior, performance optimizations, and interactive features, every aspect of the player can be configured or extended.

This flexibility makes it possible to support a wide range of use cases—from simple video playback to rich, interactive, and data-driven experiences—while maintaining consistency across platforms and devices. The result is a player that fits seamlessly into existing applications and workflows, rather than forcing teams to adapt to a fixed experience.

Audio Player

The Kaltura Audio Player provides a streamlined way to deliver audio-only content with optimal performance and accessibility. By loading a lightweight player tailored for audio, it reduces bandwidth usage and page load impact compared to full video players, making it ideal for podcasts, lectures, and on-the-go listening.

Thumbnail Player

The main advantage of using thumbnail embeds on Kaltura players is performance optimization. By loading a lightweight thumbnail instead of the full player on initial page load, the player is only initialized when the user interacts with it. This significantly reduces network traffic, improves page load time, and lowers resource consumption, especially on pages with multiple videos or for users on limited bandwidth or mobile networks.

Quiz Player

The Kaltura Quiz Player enhances video from a passive viewing experience into an interactive learning moment. By embedding questions directly into the video, it keeps viewers engaged, reinforces key concepts in real time, and improves knowledge retention. Quizzes encourage active participation, help validate understanding, and provide immediate feedback to learners.

Call to Actions Inside the Player

Calls to Action (CTAs) inside the video player turn viewer attention into meaningful outcomes at the moment of highest engagement. By presenting prompts directly within the video—such as links, buttons, or next steps—CTAs reduce friction and guide viewers to take action without leaving the experience.

Hotspots Inside the Player

Hotspots inside the video player add an interactive layer that transforms video from a linear experience into an exploratory one. By placing clickable or informational elements directly on the video at precise moments, hotspots let viewers access additional context, resources, or actions without interrupting playback.

Video with Extended Audio Description

Extended audio description enhances accessibility by providing richer, more detailed narration of visual elements that are essential to understanding the content. When enabled on the player, it allows additional descriptive audio to be inserted—often with brief pauses in playback—so viewers who are blind or visually impaired can fully follow on-screen actions, context, and visual cues.

By supporting extended audio description, the player helps organizations meet accessibility standards while delivering a more inclusive and equitable viewing experience. It ensures that visual storytelling, demonstrations, and on-screen information are accessible to all audiences without compromising comprehension or content quality.

Dual Player

Kaltura supports dual player functionality, enabling two synchronized video streams to play simultaneously within a single player. This is ideal for use cases such as sign language interpretation, picture-in-picture presentations, or multi-angle recordings.

With dual players, viewers can watch the main content while also seeing a secondary feed—like a sign language interpreter—without missing any part of the original video. This enhances accessibility, inclusivity, and engagement, while giving content owners the flexibility to deliver richer, more user-friendly viewing experiences.

Player with Banner Overlay

The banner overlay on the Kaltura player provides a clear and non-intrusive way to communicate important information directly within the viewing experience. By displaying contextual messages—such as disclaimers—on top of the video, banners ensure visibility without requiring viewers to leave or interrupt playback.

Player with Captions

Kaltura player captions provide accessible text for video and audio. Key features include multi-language support, in-video search, and customizable appearance.

Player with Summary & Chapters

Kaltura's Summary & Chapters feature, powered by AI (Content Lab), enhances video navigation by automatically generating searchable, clickable segments and content overviews. It supports manual editing of timestamps, titles, and descriptions, allowing users to create custom, interactive bookmarks for long-form content.
